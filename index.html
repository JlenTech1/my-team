ABOUT KAPASA MAKASA UNIVERSITY LIBRARY

We champion knowledge to empower academic success and societal growth.

At Kapasa Makasa University Library, we are committed to fostering academic excellence and innovation. Established in 2019 under Copperbelt University and becoming fully independent in 2021, the library is located along the Great North Road in Chinsali District, Muchinga Province.

Our mission is to support learning, teaching, and research by providing access to vital academic resources and a welcoming environment. We collaborate with faculty and students to develop skills, promote access to knowledge, and contribute to national and global educational development.

With a vision to become a leading center of knowledge, the library embraces technology, inclusivity, and lifelong learning to empower our academic community.
